How to properly cite sources

Depending on the style of citation you use there will be different ways to cite different types of sources. In most cases books, journal articles, websites, and other types of sources require different formatting. Below are examples of different types of citation, in-text and bibliographic, for common sources.

MLA

Book

In-text citation: (Corn, 49)

Corn, Wanda M. The Great American Thing: Modern Art and National Identity, 1915-1935. Berkeley: University of California, 1999. Print

Journal Article

Griffin, Farah. "Thirty Years of Black American Literature and Literary Studies: A Review." Journal of Black Studies. 35.2 (2004): 165-174. Print.

Website

Miranda, Carolina. "Seeing Red at MoMA." ARTnews. ARTnews LLC, 09 Feb. 2012. Web. 08 May 2013.

Chicago

Book

Chicago style uses footnotes or endnotes for in-text citations.1

1Wanda Corn, The Great American Thing: Modern Art and National Identity, 1915-1935, (Berkeley: University of California, 1999): 49.↑

Corn, Wanda M. The Great American Thing: Modern Art and National Identity, 1915-1935. Berkeley: University of California, 1999.

Journal Article

Griffin, Farah. "Thirty Years of Black American Literature and Literary Studies: A Review." Journal of Black Studies 35 (2004): 165-174.

Website

Miranda, Carolina. "Seeing Red at MoMA." ARTnews. Last Modified, February 9th, 2012. Accessed 08 May 2013. http://www.artnews.com/2012/02/09/seeing-red-at-moma-2/

APA

Examples taken from University of Maryland University College, APA Citation Examples accessed May 15, 2013.

Book

In-text citation: (Jans, 1993, p. 26)

Jans, N. (1993). The last light breaking: Life among Alaska's Inupiat Eskimos. Anchorage, AK: Alaska Northwest Books.

Article

Sagarin, B. J., & Lawler-Sagarin, K. A. (2005). Critically evaluating competing theories: An exercise based on the Kitty Genovese murder. Teaching of Psychology, 32(3), 167–169.

Website

Sea Turtle Restoration Project. (2006). Threats to sea turtles. Retrieved from http://seaturtles.org/section.php?id=104
