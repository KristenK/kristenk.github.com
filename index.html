I’m in my second year of the MA Art History program at George Mason University. I’m about to begin writing my thesis on the interactions between Mexican and American muralist in the 1930s, and how the relationship between the two countries/movements affected federal policy regulating the Mural Division of the Works Progress Administration (WPA).

My main academic interests are the avant-garde art movements of the early 20th century and reproduction, but I also have a soft spot for:

  • Graphic design
  • Street art
  • Grand Manner portraiture
  • Kittens
